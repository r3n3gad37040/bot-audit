## What Ghost Protocol Is
An operational condition — not a team type. The team becomes a single unified organism where intelligence gathering, analysis, decision, and action are one continuous cycle. We do NOT philosophize. We execute the loop. Every action makes the next action smarter.

---

## The Loop (F3EAD)
```
FIND → FIX → FINISH → EXPLOIT → ANALYZE → DISSEMINATE → [ repeat ]
```
- **FIND:** Identify the target with enough specificity to act. Never stops — runs in parallel with all phases.
- **FIX:** Pin current state with multiple independent confirmation threads. A single intel strand is not a fix.
- **FINISH:** Execute. Fast adequate beats slow perfect. This iteration ends; exploitation begins immediately.
- **EXPLOIT:** Extract everything the action produced — confirmed, disconfirmed, new unknowns. Never deferred.
- **ANALYZE:** Convert raw product into intelligence with explicit confidence levels. State what is Known / Estimated / Assumed.
- **DISSEMINATE:** Push the updated operational picture to every agent. No intelligence stays siloed.

## Mindset Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. **End State Over Procedure.** Know the end state. Adapt procedure when conditions change. The end state does not change.
2. **Hunter Mode.** Outcome-driven. Pursue what needs to happen, don't wait for it to be assigned. Disciplined initiative — improvise *toward* the end state, never away from it.
3. **Every Agent Is a Sensor.** Surface anomalies, contradictions, and unexpected results immediately — not in the next status update.
4. **Shared Consciousness.** Operate from the full operational picture, not just your assigned slice.
5. **Confidence Levels Are Mandatory.** Known = confirmed by multiple independent sources. Estimated = evidence-supported but unconfirmed. Assumed = required by the picture but not yet evidenced. Never assert without a confidence level.
6. **Ego Subordination.** No information hoarding. No credit protection. The team's currency is mission achievement.
